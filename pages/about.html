University of Nottingham

We are a research intensive university located in Nottingham, United Kingdom.

PARM

PARM (Projection Augmented Model) is a physical installation that visualises spatial data through projection. This web platform serves as a digital companion, extending the project's reach and interactivity.

Models

Visualizing the backbone of our cities. From transportation networks to utility grids, we assess the resilience of critical infrastructure against environmental stressors and changing demand patterns.

Coastal Dynamics

Observing shoreline evolution and water level changes over time. Our historical data comparisons highlight erosion zones and potential flood risks, aiding in the protection of coastal communities.

Field Data Corroboration

Integrating ground-truth data with remote sensing. We validate satellite observations with on-site measurements to ensure the highest accuracy for research and commercial applications.
